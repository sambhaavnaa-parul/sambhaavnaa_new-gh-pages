Set in the village of Kandwari, in the lap of the mighty Dhauladhar mountain ranges in Himachal Pradesh, is a unique learning and living space for those concerned with social and political change. The Sambhaavnaa Institute of Public Policy and Politics was founded under aegis of the Kumud Bhushan Education Society in 2004. Our main mission is to nurture value-based leadership by encouraging individuals, especially the youth; to reflect, discuss and develop a critical perspective on the ideals and ideas that define a just society.

Campus Philosophy

A description of the Sambhaavnaa campus would be incomplete without talking about the farm, cowshed and tea-gardens on the campus which provide us with organic food resources for our own consumption. A seed bank is being built to collect and conserve a wide variety of local food varieties from across the region.

Built essentially using local materials - wood, bamboo, earth and stone, with a traditional architectural design and practices by a set of local carpenters, masons and workers, the campus has been designed by Didi Contractor, a renowned architect specialising in traditional eco-friendly construction methods of the Himalayas. The campus has been built using eco-friendly and sustainable technology and practices that it is attempting to promote. The spirit of Sambhaavnaa is captured in the fact that it is run and managed by a small team of local staff and a series of local and visiting volunteers and associates. These are individuals who have a background in the social sector or just those exploring alternative and sustainable lifestyles.

Our Beliefs

We belive that there is a need to question and challenge the inequities in social, economic and political structures and their dominance by vested interests.

That there is a need to radically change the structure and functioning of the society, polity and the economy to serve public interest with the principles of equality, justice and freedom.

That this change has three essential components:

-Strengthening of Resistance and Political Action
-Supporting and Working on Creation of Alternatives (across the socio-economic-political models/institutions)
-Bringing about Personal Transformation

For the above, there is a need for spaces that will:

- Facilitate collaboration, exchange and innovation in strategies and action
- Facilitate the building of a politically conscious citizenry especially youth
- Facilitate the process of personal transformation


Sambhaavnaa is such a space.
