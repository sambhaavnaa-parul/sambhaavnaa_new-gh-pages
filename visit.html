Visiting Protocol

Sambhaavnaa Campus is open 7 days a week and you are welcome to visit between 10:00 - 18:00. We are always happy to receive visitors who would like to see our campus, but if you wish to visit us, please note that we need to be informed at least a week in advance from the date of the visit (via email or phone) so that we are prepared to meet you. Sambhaavnaa encourages day visitors who are connected to organizations (those who wish to stay a night or two). Please note that boarding and lodging costs may apply.The vistors are encourged to follow the campus norms listed here.

How to reach?

Bus: Sambhaavnaa and Udaan are located in Kandbari that falls between between Palampur and Baijnath. There are overnight buses (Volvo, AC, Non-AC Deluxe and Ordinary) from New Delhi to Palampur/Baijnath leaving daily in the evening from Delhi. They depart from the Inter State Bus Terminal (ISBT) at Kashmiri Gate, some buses also pick up passengers from the Tibetan colony, Majnu ka Tilla in North Delhi. Tickets can be booked online at http://hrtc.gov.in/hrtctickets/. Tickets can also be booked in advance at the ISBT bus station at the Himachal Transport or Haryana Roadways counters in the ticket hall. The overnight journey takes approximately 10-12 hours, arriving in Palampur early in the morning. The bus route is Delhi – Palampur – Baijnath. If the bus terminates in Palampur, you can take a bus/taxi to the village of Kandbari. If the bus travels onwards to Baijnath, ask to be dropped at Dhramman, Kandbari is 3km from Dhramman. You can either wait for a bus headed uphill or hail a cab waiting in the vicinity.
Train: Many trains run throughout India to Pathankot or Chakki Bank stations on the line to Jammu Tawi. There are several daily overnight trains from Delhi to Pathankot/Chakki Bank. Timetables and fares can be checked on www.indianrail.gov.in. Kandbari is then a 3.5-hour taxi ride from Pathankot. You may try using http://erail.in. We have found it to be quite efficient.
Air: There is a daily flight service from New Delhi to Dharamsala (Gaggal-Kangra Airport). Kandbari is 1.5 hours by taxi from the airport. Please note that these flights are sometimes cancelled due to adverse weather conditions or under-booking.

Note: There are other Dhramans in the district. Please ensure while boarding the bus that you are traveling to the Dhraman closer to Palampur.
