Sambhaavnaa Institute welcomes volunteers and fellows to the campus in order to create an atmosphere that is mutually nourishing. Hostel facilities housing comfortable dormitories and a kitchenette facility are available for the volunteers and fellows to avail of.

Volunteering

Depending on the time of the year and the Sambhaavnaa schedule, you can expect a volunteer pool of four to eight individuals working at the campus. Sambhaavnaa accepts volunteers at all times of the year except the Winter Break (Jan 15- Feb 15) and the Monsoon Break (July 15- Aug 15).

Sambhaavnaa prefers long term volunteers and expects a minimum commitment of one month from all the volunteers who are expected to abide by the vision and ethos of the institute. There are various ways in which volunteers can engage with the Sambhaavnaa Institute. Please fill the volunteer form (Given Below) for our review, and we will get in touch with you based on our requirements.

Please note that the Sambhaavnaa campus is a vegetarian campus and also has a no smoking/ no alcohol policy.

Fellowships

A minimum commitment of three months is expected out of an individual who wishes to be associated with Sambhaavnaa as a Fellow. Depending on availability and qualifications, the individual will then be intimated about his/her selection.
