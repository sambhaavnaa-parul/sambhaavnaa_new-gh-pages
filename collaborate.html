Sambhaavnaa is an open space for everyone to share and gain knowledge. Sambhaavnaa Institute has been collaborating with like-minded organisations In such programs, the resources and responsibilities are shared between Sambhaavnaa and the collaborating organisation.

How to collaborate?

Sambhaavnaa Institute organise collaborative programs with like-minded organisations/individuals regularly. In such programs, the resources and responsibilities are shared between Sambhaavnaa and the collaborating organisation/individual. If you or your organisation wants to collabrate with Sambhaavnaa please contact the programs team at programs@sambhaavnaa.org.

Host your event at Sambhaavnaa

Sambhaavnaa Institute is also open to hosting programs with likeminded organisations can organise their own issue-based workshops, organisational meetings or retreats at the Institute’s campus. Sambhaavnaa will charge the costs for lodging and boarding facilities as well as other campus facilities for such programs.

For more information write to us at programs@sambhaavnaa.org

OR call us at +91 8894227954

We accept donations in the form of sponsorship for participants of the programs organised by/at the Institute. Sambhaavnaa offers a stipulated number of fee waivers across various workshops, programs and events for candidates that do not have the financial means for it. To sponsor a participant/learning journey for any of our upcoming programs, write to us at – programs@sambhaavnaa.org
