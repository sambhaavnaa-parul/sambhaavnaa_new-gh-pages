Program Reports


Sambhaavnaa Institute organises 20-30 workshops and programs around the year. The programs and workshops are conducted according to set pedagogy and is, simultaneously, flexible so as to suit the kind of participation involved. This creates a unique and different experience in every workshop. We document these experiences with the help of our faciliators, resource persons and participants and create programs report for every workshop. The program reports gives a glimpse of experiences, activites, discussions and outcomes of the workshop.
